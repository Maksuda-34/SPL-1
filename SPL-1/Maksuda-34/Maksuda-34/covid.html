What Are The Main Symptoms?

COVID-19 affects different people in different ways. Most infected people will develop mild to moderate illness and recover without hospitalization

    Most Common Symptomps

  • fever
  • Tiredness
  • Dry Cough

    • Less Common Symptomps

  • Sore Throat
  • Aches and Pains
  • Headache
  • Conjunctivitis
  • Discolouration of fingers or toes

    • Serious Symptomps

  • Shortness of Breath
  • Chest Pain or Pressure
  • Loss of Speech or Movement

things not to do during covid

Avoid touching surfaces, especially in public settings or health facilities, in case people infected with COVID-19 have touched them. Clean surfaces regularly with standard disinfectants.

  • Do Not Share Eating
  • Do Not Touch Your Face or Nose
  • Do Not Contact Sick People

things to do during covid

For being safe from COVID-19 things have to do are keeping hands clean, practising sneezing and coughing into bent elbows, opening windows, wearing a mask if age-appropriate, and continuing physical distancing.

  • Wash Your Hands For 20 sec
  • Wear a Mask if Available
